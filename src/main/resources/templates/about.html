Expense Tracker

Welcome to our Expense Tracker and Friends Expenses project. This platform is designed to help you manage your personal expenses and track shared expenses with friends effortlessly.

Mission is to provide an intuitive and efficient solution for tracking finances and managing group expenses, making financial tracking a hassle-free experience.

  • Expense Tracking

    Log your daily expenses and categorize them for a comprehensive overview.

  • Group Expenses

    Easily split bills and track shared expenses when out with friends or in a group.

  • User-Friendly Interface

    A simple and intuitive interface for a seamless user experience.

  • Technologies Used

    I utilized cutting-edge technologies including Java Spring Boot, Thymeleaf, Bootstrap, and MySQL for a robust and efficient platform.
